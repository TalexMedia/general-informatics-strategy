03 The Pattern

You've Already Built The Hard Part

The thread that connects everything above.

Three books. Two seasons of a CEO video series. A weekly blog with named authors. 25 years in business. 6,300 LinkedIn followers. An acquisition closed two weeks ago. The CEO is on camera, in print, and on an industry advisory board.

Almost no MSP can say any of this. The work is already in the ground.

What happened after Season 2 of The Grind wrapped in November 2023 is the actual pattern. The video show stalled. Episodes migrated from geninf.com to itstrategy.tech. Customer stories stayed in text. The AI story, the IMS Solutions Group acquisition, which is GI's real differentiator for the next three years, became a press release and a blog post, not a Grind episode.

The gap is not that GI isn't doing content. It's that GI built momentum and let it freeze. The opportunity here is revive, elevate, consolidate. Not invent.

Don addresses The Grind's existing audience. The hard industry question: most MSPs can't earn the sentence Debbie said about us. Why trust isn't a feature and can't be bought. The viewer lands on: could my current IT partner earn this sentence?

One transferable lesson. A three-question diagnostic the viewer can take into their next vendor meeting. Vendor-agnostic. Don's closing line is an invitation to hold every partner, General Informatics included, to the same standard.
